Kazuko Nakamura

The first Japanese female animator.

Kazuko Nakamura in the early days of Anime.

Animes she worked on:
  • Alakazam the Great (movie): Animation
  • Alice in Wonderland (TV): Character Design
  • (The) Big O (TV): Background Art (Biho)
  • Chiisana Jumbo (movie): Animation
  • Cleopatra (movie): Animation
  • Hakujaden (movie): Animation
  • Jack to Mame no Ki (movie): Animation
  • Kamichu! (TV): Publicity
  • Magic Boy (movie): Animation
  • Manga Nihon Mukashi Banashi (TV '76): Background Art
  • Marvelous Melmo (TV): Art
  • Phoenix 2772 - Space Firebird (movie): Art, Animation Director
  • (The) Phoenix: Chapter of Dawn (movie): Art
  • Princess Knight (TV): Character Design, Art Director, Animation Director
  • Senya Ichiya Monogatari (movie): Art
  • Tales of the Street Corner (movie): Art, Animation
  • Wonder Three (TV): Animation (Bokko)
  • World's Famous Stories for Children: Thumb Princess (movie): Assistant Animation Director
  • Yasashii Lion (movie): Animation
Anime she was cast in:
  • (The) Tatami Galaxy (TV) as Honwaka Girl D (ep 5)

Having been trained at Toei Doga, she brought to Mushi Pro a precious commodity - an approach grounded in the fundamentals of how to move characters. She was a role model both as a powerhouse animator and as a strong female figure in the workplace.
